We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, improve, and protect ZimTechHub services. Your information may be used for the following purposes:

✅ Process digital marketplace transactions
✅ Provide customer support
✅ Improve website functionality and performance
✅ Detect fraud, abuse, and security threats
✅ Communicate important platform updates
✅ Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies & Analytics

ZimTechHub uses cookies and similar technologies to provide a better user experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences, maintain login sessions, improve security, and understand how users interact with our platform.

Essential Cookies

Required for authentication, security, and core website functionality.

Preference Cookies

Remember settings such as preferences and user choices.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand usage and improve our platform.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of ZimTechHub may not function correctly.

You may request deletion of your ZimTechHub account and associated personal information by contacting our support team. Some information may be retained where necessary for legal obligations, security purposes, preventing fraud, or maintaining transaction records.

ZimTechHub is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that such information has been collected, appropriate steps will be taken to remove it.
